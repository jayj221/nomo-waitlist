How a connection happens

01

You're placed, privately

Upload your photos and our AI places you in a bracket. You see your placement exactly once. Nobody else ever does, and neither do you again. You only ever meet people in your own bracket.

02

Ten people a day

Every morning: ten people, ranked one to ten by how much your vibe overlaps: ideas, mentality, music. Their name, their music, their hobbies, their honest answers. Everything except their face. Read them all. Mark who you'd talk to.

03

One window. One hour.

At a random moment each day, everyone's phone lights up at once. The window is open for one hour. Mutual picks connect live. Voice first, text if you'd rather. Start with your number one, and if the vibe isn't there, move down the list. If the hour ends and you both want more, you keep each other: a real match you can talk to every day.

04

Two reveals a day

When a call feels like something, ask for the reveal. If they ask too, photos appear for both of you at once. You get two reveals a day. Spend them like they matter, because they do.

05

One more yes

You've seen each other. One last question: still them? Yes makes it your first match, with chat and socials shared on your terms. No, and you move down your list to whoever's next.
